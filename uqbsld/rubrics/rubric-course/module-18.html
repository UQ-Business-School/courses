Apply Design Frameworks: VoI + SOLO

Take the contextualised descriptor and apply VoI (Depth/Breadth/Cogency) and SOLO (Depth progression) to make it fully observable. This draws on everything from Modules 5–9.

Frameworks to apply: Variation of Importance (Depth, Breadth, Cogency) and SOLO taxonomy (Unistructural → Multistructural → Relational → Extended Abstract).

"Analyses root causes of the turnover problem by examining underlying assumptions (Depth). Synthesises across 3+ theoretical perspectives (Breadth), resolving where they conflict (Cogency).
