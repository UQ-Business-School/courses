Remove copyrighted PDFs

UQ Library licences do not permit courses to upload Journal Articles, Textbooks or Textbook Chapters directly into Blackboard. Use Talis, the HBR LTI, or the Library's EZproxy link generator instead.

Open Talis guide UQ Library EZproxy Generator

Find your lecture recordings

Your EchoVideo lecture recordings sit inside Blackboard's Course Resources area. Use the course menu to reach the linked recording space.

  1. These steps must be completed by the Course Coordinator.
  2. Click Course Resources in the course menu.
  3. Click Lecture Recordings.
  4. Use the dropdown list to select your current course offering. Contact eLearning if your course appears missing from the dropdown menu.

This is the little link that causes big Week 1 confusion when it is missed. Fix it now; future-you gets a tiny parade.
